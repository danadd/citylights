About The Album

City Lights is composed of six tracks with their own unique style and sound. Though each track offers something new and unique, they all mesh together in a beautiful sound. Tracks such as Ice Queen let the listener relax to Baekhyun's smooth, low register while Betcha will find itself on the houseparty playlist.

There are two versions: Day version and Night version. Both musical CDs are the same, but the contents of the album booklet, photocards, and album design is different. Please see below for the differences.
