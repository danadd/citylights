About

Considering taking a vacation to South Korea? Need a quick and convenient way to pay for public transportation? A Cashbee card may be right for you!

Top up this card at your local 7/11, GS25, or Cashbee Kiosk with Korean won (cash only). Once loaded, simply tap this card on designated areas on the bus, subway station, or taxi. You can even use this card to pay for items at convenient stores!

This latest version comes in two designs: Day Version and Night Version to make Baekhyun's 1st solo album. Both cards work in the same way and only differ by design.
