About
The Artist

Byun Baekhyun is the lead vocalist of the nine member Korean-Chinese group, EXO. Debuting in 2012 through a series of 12 teasers, he has shown his vocal prowess over the years through various group and solo activities. With the group's top selling hit, Growl, in 2013 they were able to expand into the global market to reach more fans and admirers.

Baekhyun isn't only known for his work in EXO. He is also a member of the subunit EXO-CBX (comprised of fellow group members Chen and Xiumin), the leader of K-Pop super group, SuperM, and even has released his own solo album, City Lights. Each unit offers a different sound ranging from refreshing ballads, catchy pop, and chill R&B, showcasing his ability to adapt to any concept or sound.

Baekhyun's talents don't end at him being a singer- he has become an all-around entertainer. He has appeared in a variety of acting roles, participated in songwriting, and is the co-creative director of his own fashion line, Prive Alliance/Prive by BBH. He has won numerous awards throughout the years, several which can be found below.
